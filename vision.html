Ambitions and Future Prospects


"Throughout a person's life, learning is one thing that should stay constant." This is what I firmly believe in. By reflecting on your past misadventures, one can learn to grow and strive towards a better future. Since I gained a firm grip over my own choices, Computers have been a constant source of fascination for me. Gradually as I am learning new things, I feel delighted at my past experiences of failing a task 100 times before succeeding. This has formed the foundation of my knowledge gathering process.

I am currently interested in exploring the field of Artificial Intelligence to its depth to find its implications on the development of computers in the future. It is the new era of technology where we are changing our ideologies and perceptions towards the basic functioning of computer programming. I want to learn as much as I can from these advancements to contribute my own part in the progressing world.
